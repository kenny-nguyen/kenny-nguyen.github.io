ABOUT


Kathy Tran - Senior Stylist / Senior Colorist

Bean is often believed to be my name. However, my name is actually Kathy! "Bean" comes from Javabean, my precious french bulldog.

More information about me:

Two months after birth in Vietnam, Kathy and her family immigrated to Vancouver, Canada. After 7 years, she moved to Calgary, Alberta where she practiced and began her career in hair styling. After seven years of working in Calgary, Kathy moved to Toronto where she currently works.

Kathy is a Senior Stylist and Colorist at Lisa Dinh Hair Studio in Toronto, Canada - Visit the salon at 651 Dupont St, Toronto, Ontario M6G 1Z4.

Kathy's favorite part of her career is the variety of beautiful clients she gets to meet and work with. Her mission to make others feel confident and empowering creates an unforgettable experience that extends to more beyond just hair.

Fun fact: Kathy is the oldest of nine!
